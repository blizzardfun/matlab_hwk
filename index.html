Pharmaceutical Analysis

By C Thompson

Pharmaceutical homework problem using data from csv files read into Pandas dataframes with results of analysis plotted using Matplotlib.

After reading the csv data files, the data on each mouse and on clinical measurements of response to drug treatment, the average tumor volume measurements were grouped by drug and days treatment. The standard error of the mean was calculated followed by reformatting and then graphing of the results including error bars for each data point.



Using the merged data, the measurements of the average number of metastatic sites were grouped by drug and days treatment. The standard error of the mean was calculated followed by reformatting and then graphing of the results including error bars for each data point.


Using the count of surviving mice in the study the count was grouped by drug and days treatment. It was then reformatted and the counts were graphed for each drug and the days treatment.


The percentage change in tumor volume for the entire study for each drug was calculated and grouped into passing for a decrease in tumor size and failing for increased tumor size. The percentage change in tumor size for each drug is shown on a bar graph with the percentage value printed on the bar for ease of comparison
